Lo-Fi

Lo-Fi is a relativley new genre. It is good to learn especially if you want to learn to sample. Many of these samples are jazz, which is often the hardest type of music to sample due to much of it not being centered around 4/4 time. Lo-Fi also teaches how to use drum loops, audio effects, balancing, and is friendly to one wanting to add extra insturments.
