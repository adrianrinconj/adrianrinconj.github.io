Jazz

Jazz is definetly a more niche genre of music. Jazz would be the hardest to produce over software, as much of the appeal of jazz comes from its "human" elements such as spontaneity and improvisation within the moment. When jazz is incorporated in modern day music, it is mostly due to samples, drum loops, or instruments such as saxophones and trumpets. Lo-Fi incorporates a lot of jazz.
