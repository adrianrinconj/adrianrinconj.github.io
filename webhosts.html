Web Hosts Review

godaddy.com

Godaddy's website hosting is the biggest website hosting in the world. It has a starting price of "Economy" that is $5.99 a month up to a Maximum plan that is $19.99 a month. The hosting services that it has are 24/7 support, SSL Certificate, and Bussiness hosting plans and Wordpress Hosting Plans (Wordpress hosting plan has its own seperate payment). You can get more than a dozen domain names, and hundreds of sub domians aswell.

BlueHost.com

Bluehost's website hosting actually has a sale going on right now. It usually makes you pay $8.99 a month, but is going on sale for $2.95 a month as of now. This also has the SSL Certificate included, with a year free domain thrown in when you sign up. BlueHost is also known for having good speed for a web host and has unlimited domain hosting.

hostgator.com

HostGator is also a top competetor for best website hosts. It starts off at $2.75 a month at the "Hatchling Plan", and goes up to $5.25 a month for the "Bussiness Plan". You also get an SSL Certificate with this website, aswell as 24/7 costumer service. This website is also uses Shared Hosting, and has more than a dozen domains and many more sub-domains that you can choose from.

My Personal Pick

My personal pick out of these three would be HostGator. I would choose this because this seems to be the cheapest for its highest quality package, and has the most accessibility features suck as, one click installers and unmetered storage.
